Resources for Learning Chinese

Reading

  • Chinese Reading Practice

    A collection of reading materials for different levels to practice Chinese reading skills.

  • Mandarin Companion

    Graded readers designed to help learners improve their Chinese through engaging stories.

Writing

  • Skritter

    An app that helps you learn how to write Chinese characters with proper stroke order.

  • Arch Chinese

    A comprehensive tool for learning Chinese characters, including stroke order and character etymology.

Listening

  • ChinesePod

    A podcast-based learning platform with lessons for all levels to improve listening skills.

  • Maayot

    Daily Chinese stories delivered to your inbox to help you practice listening and reading.

Speaking

  • italki

    A platform to find language tutors for one-on-one speaking practice and lessons.

  • Tandem

    An app to find language exchange partners for practicing speaking with native speakers.
