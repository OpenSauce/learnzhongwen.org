About

Hi! I'm a software engineer who has been learning Chinese for 10 years. I'm building this site to help me learn and to share all the things that have helped me. I hope you find it useful too.

This site is designed to provide resources and tools for learning Chinese, including reading, writing, listening, and speaking practice. Regardless level, I hope you'll find something useful here.

Features

  • Curated reading materials for different levels
  • Writing practice with stroke order guidance
  • Listening exercises with native speakers
  • Speaking practice with language exchange partners
